Site Intent

The Angelfire.com re-design project will focus its efforts on creating a more usable and aesthetically pleasing user interface. We aim to clarify and make obvious the site's 3 main goals of offering users the following:

  1. A means to easily create websites
  2. Domain name & email addresses
  3. Website hosting services

Using clear entry points, we will be bringing Angelfire.com into the realm of modern web design. They will regain their lost marketshare in the template based website creation service industry.

Competitive Analysis

According to our research, Angelfire has has a few main competitors at the moment. The most formidable of which is weebly.com, so we'll be devoting our attention to analyzing their current strategy and market share. We have found that our competitor is thriving, proving the template-based free website business is still a lucrative venture at this time.

Site Statistics

Weebly.com currently has an Alexa.com global traffic rank of 310. Weebly's current success in the marketplace is likely due to an aggressive backling strategy that has propelled them higher up in the Google.com search results. Currently, Weebly has 221,095 back-links pointing to their site & services. This is against Angelfire's 100,438 back links. The average user views 2.81 pages when they visit Weebly for an average time-on-site of 4:43. This suggests users are finding what they are looking for and are completing their task efficiently. Their bounce rate is a low 43.5%.

Demographics

The age of a typical Weebly.com user is, on average, 45-65+. These folks posess some college education, are mostly male, has children, and are mostly browsing from home. This suggests that the audience for the template-based free website service is an older crowd, looking to make personal or home based business websites.

Personas

Our typical users can be personified by Inga & Gerry.

Inga is a cinical, 64 year old widow. Her children are in their 40s, have their own children, live in other states, and visit once per year on holidays. Inga has started a home based business selling Mary Kay and needs an easy to maintain website to direct people to from her business cards.

Inga is familiar with computers to the extent that she checks her email daily, forwards jokes to her friends, and occasionally hops on Facebook to post comments about what may have gone wrong in her day. Inga is easily frustrated, and has a slow internet connection at home. Any website she uses must be light, and easy to use. She is easily confused by an abundance of information. inga does not use a smartphone, and anytime she has a tech problem, she calls her gardner.

Inga is essentially retired and is involved in Mary Kay to essentially fight off Alzheimers. She attends church weekly, and tends to be a bit of a busy-body around coffee hour at said church.

Inga's workflow is this:

  1. Sit down with coffee
  2. Turn on her Windows OS desktop computer
  3. Open her web browser
  4. Navigate to website by entering URL in Yahoo search bar
  5. Perform task (check email, Facebook, Yahoo news, or check her Mary Kay website)
  6. Upon completion, close Internet Explorer
  7. Power off computer

Gerry is a married, 48 year old man. He enjoys poker night with some old friends and his website needs are mainly to post pictures of welding projects he has completed. He is proud of his sculptures so he likes to show his relatives, children (he has two college age children), and friends.

Gerry is familiar with computers to the extent that he checks his email daily, watches TED videos, surfs news sites, uses Facebook, and occasionally receives dirty jokes in his email, from friends.

Gerry works at a machine shop, has health insurance, gets paid time off and likes to travel the U.S. in his RV with his wife Linda.

Gerry's workflow is this:

  1. Crack open a beer after work
  2. Sit down at his wife's kitchen iMac
  3. Edit photos he has taken of his works-in-progress
  4. Post said pictures to his personal website and Facebook
  5. Browse news sites, or watch a TED video until dinner is ready
